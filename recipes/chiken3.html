five spice chicken with cucumber salad

RECIPE NOTES

Tip: Want to save time on the washing up? Instead of pan-frying, warm the tortillas in the microwave following packet directions.

  • 8 mini flour tortillas
  • 350g coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup Thai basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Step 1
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C/200°C fan forced. Arrange chicken burger patties (400g packet crumbed chicken burger patties) on a baking tray. Bake, turning halfway, for 15 minutes or until cooked through. Cut each burger patty into 8 slices.
  3. Step 2
  4. Meanwhile, heat a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Spray with oil. Cook the tortillas for about 30 seconds each side or until golden. Transfer to a plate and cover with a tea towel. Combine just the coleslaw mix (save the dressing sachet for another use) and lime juice in a bowl.
  5. Step 3
  6. Top tortillas with slaw mixture. Top with burger slices, avocado, tonkatsu sauce and Kewpie mayonnaise. Sprinkle with coriander, to serve
